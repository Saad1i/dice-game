Rules for the Game

1. Roll the dice and the number will be added to your current score.

2. If you roll a one, you lose your current score and the round switches to your opponent.

3. If you are happy with your current score, click on hold and save your current score. And the round will switch to your opponent.

4. Play until one of you have a score of at least 100.

5. When the game ends, if you want to play again, simply click on new round to keep playing.

6. If you want to start an entirely new game, click on new game.
